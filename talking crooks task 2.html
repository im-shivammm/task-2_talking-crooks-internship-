01

Clinic Management Software

Business Reason — Most clinics still operate on paper/WhatsApp, causing missed appointments, lost records, and billing errors that cost revenue and patient trust daily.

Problem Statement

Independent clinics and small hospitals in India rely on physical registers, loose paper prescriptions, and WhatsApp messages to manage appointments and patient history. This leads to frequent appointment clashes, misplaced prescription files, manual billing errors, and zero follow-up visibility. Doctors spend time on paperwork instead of patients, and the lack of a digital record makes audits and reports impossible. A centralized clinic management system would eliminate these gaps entirely — enabling any staff member to book, bill, and retrieve records in seconds.

Target Users

Primary: Clinic receptionist (appointment + billing)
Secondary: Doctor (patient records, prescriptions)
Admin/Owner: Daily reports, staff management

Business Size: Solo to 5-doctor clinics, diagnostics labs, dental/eye clinics.


Why This Business Would Invest

A ₹15,000–₹40,000/yr software subscription easily pays for itself by recovering even one missed appointment per day. Patient retention improves with automated reminders, and insurance/GST billing compliance reduces legal risk.

Key Features (minimum 5)

  • Appointment Calendar — Book, reschedule, cancel with SMS/WhatsApp reminder auto-sent to patient
  • Patient Records & History — Digital profile with visit log, diagnosis history, allergies, vitals
  • Digital Prescription — Doctor types medicine + dosage; printable PDF generated instantly
  • Billing & Invoicing — GST-compliant invoice generation, multiple payment modes, outstanding dues tracker
  • Reports & Analytics — Daily/weekly revenue, top diagnoses, patient visit trends
  • Role-Based Access — Receptionist, doctor, and admin each see only what they need

Business Value

  • Zero double-bookings → happier patients, fewer walkouts
  • Digital prescriptions reduce pharmacy miscommunication errors
  • Automated billing reduces revenue leakage by 20–35%
  • Patient history at fingertips speeds up each consultation
  • Compliance-ready reports for GST, insurance, and audits

02

Restaurant Billing & Order System

Business Reason — Manual order-taking causes kitchen confusion, missed items, slow billing, and zero sales data, costing restaurants 15–30% in avoidable losses daily.

Problem Statement

Most dhabas, small restaurants, and cafes in India still use paper notepads for order-taking, resulting in illegible kitchen orders, forgotten items, wrong bills, and slow table turnover. There is no way to track which table is occupied, what was ordered, or what's pending in the kitchen. The owner has no visibility into daily revenue or best-selling items without manually tallying receipts. A digital table + KOT + billing system would reduce errors by 80%, speed up service, and give the owner real-time business data.

Target Users

Waiter/Server: Takes orders on tablet/phone at table
Kitchen Staff: Sees live KOT (Kitchen Order Ticket) on screen
Cashier: Generates & settles final bill
Owner/Manager: Daily sales reports & menu management

Business Size: 5–50 table restaurants, cafes, cloud kitchens, food courts.


Why This Business Would Invest

Faster table turnover (even 1 extra table per shift) = higher daily revenue. Elimination of manual billing errors removes staff theft risk. Inventory data from KOT helps reduce over-purchasing. A ₹10,000–₹25,000/yr system typically recovers its cost in the first month of saved food waste alone.

Key Features (minimum 5)

  • Table Management — Visual floor map showing free/occupied/reserved tables with order status
  • Digital Menu & Order Taking — Category-wise menu with photo/price, waiter taps to add, modifiers (spice, portion size)
  • KOT (Kitchen Order Ticket) — Order fires instantly to kitchen display/printer; partial orders supported
  • Smart Billing — Auto-calculated bill with GST, discount, split bill, UPI/cash/card settlement
  • Daily Sales Dashboard — Revenue by hour, best sellers, covers count, table turnover rate
  • Menu Management — Owner marks items unavailable in real-time; price updates instantly across all devices

Business Value

  • Eliminates wrong orders and missing items → better guest experience
  • Kitchen gets legible, ordered tickets → faster food service
  • Split billing & multi-pay modes → no lost revenue from payment friction
  • Sales analytics help owner decide what to push, what to cut
  • Reduces need for extra cashier staff → direct cost savings

03

Real Estate CRM

Business Reason — Real estate agents lose 40–60% of leads due to missed follow-ups managed on WhatsApp; a dedicated CRM with reminders and property matching directly converts that to commission revenue.

Problem Statement

Independent real estate agents and small brokerages in India manage hundreds of potential buyers and sellers through a chaotic mix of WhatsApp chats, Excel sheets, and mental notes. Leads fall through the cracks because there is no follow-up system, no way to match client requirements to available properties, and no communication log to refer back to. Agents waste hours searching through chats for property details shared earlier. A purpose-built Real Estate CRM would centralize leads, properties, follow-up tasks, and client communication — directly increasing deal closure rates and reducing agent burnout.

Target Users

Real Estate Agent: Manages leads, property listings, site visits
Agency Admin: Assigns leads to agents, tracks overall pipeline
Broker/Owner: Revenue pipeline, team performance overview

Business Size: Solo agents to 30-agent brokerages; both residential and commercial segments.


Why This Business Would Invest

Even one extra deal per month from a recovered lead pays for a year of CRM subscription. Real estate margins are high — the ROI calculation is simple. Investors and HNI clients also expect professional, documented communication, making a CRM a trust signal as much as a productivity tool.

Key Features (minimum 5)

  • Lead Management & Pipeline — Kanban view: New → Contacted → Site Visit → Negotiation → Closed. Drag leads across stages
  • Property Listings — Upload photos, floor plans, price, amenities; link properties to matching client requirements
  • Auto Follow-Up Reminders — Set follow-up date; system sends in-app + WhatsApp reminder at the right time
  • Client Communication Log — All calls, WhatsApp messages, emails, and site visits logged in one thread per client
  • Document Management — Upload & store sale agreement, KYC, property docs; share with client via secure link
  • Pipeline & Revenue Reports — Deals in progress, expected revenue, closed vs. lost, agent-wise performance

Business Value

  • Never lose a lead to a missed follow-up again → direct revenue recovery
  • Property-to-client matching saves hours of manual search per day
  • Complete communication log builds professional client relationships
  • Document storage eliminates paperwork chaos and version confusion
  • Pipeline view gives broker real-time revenue forecast accuracy
